Our Story

A journey of friendship, connection, and growing love

Our story began at a mutual friend's Diwali celebration, a cloudy evening but filled with lights, laughter, and the warmth of the family. In the middle of it all, Kairav accidentally stepped on Aanya's lehenga. He was embarrased and he kept apologizing, trying to make it right with charm and humor.

What started as an awkward moment turned into a conversation that lasted the whole into the night. We laughed, teased each other, and found an unexpected ease in each other's company. That one evening led to many more weekend meet-ups, late-night conversations, and countless cups of tea together.

Now, we're here, building a life together and grateful on how it all began. Join us as we celebrate this next beautiful chapter, surrounded by the people who have been part of our journey.

"May our love never be lost, only kept, nurtured, and treasured forever."
